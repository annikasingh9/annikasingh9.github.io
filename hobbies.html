My Hobbies

Dance

Bharatnatyam dance has been a huge part of my life for 14 years. I perform recitals at the capital, religious events, and more! It connects me to my culture, and I teach it for free to young girls.

Writing

I've loved writing my whole life. It lets me express my thoughts and tell stories. I read a lot which inspires tons of what I write.

Poker

I'm disgustingly good at poker. Card games in general are my jam and go-to activity with friends if we're in a house and bored. I love strategizing and making complexities out of simple decks of cards.

Debate

Public speaking and debate are so much fun for me! I do it competitively, in my day-to-day life, and in the Youth and Government club. It teaches me so much about the world and helps me practice writing/speaking.
