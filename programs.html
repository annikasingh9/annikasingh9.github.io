Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-tac-toe is a game where two individuals using the same keyboard can play! They will be prompted to choose the index of the row (0,1,2) & then a column to pick their spot. If they make a move out of bounds, they'll be re-prompted to try again. It's such fun!

Bouncy Balls

Don't you sometimes just get sick and tired of competition and needing skills to do anything? Kick back and relax with bouncy balls, where all you do is use the arrow keys to collect balls. No risk, no reward. Stateless, simple, ideal.

Repository!

Alien Agenda

You are an alien spaceship, and the blue dots are planets soon to fall at your mercy. Hover over the planets to "conquer" them, and once you've hit 5, you spontaneously implode/disppear. You're a selfish imperialist and that's your karma. Also, avoid the flying asteroids to avoid dying instantly!
